I took part in the Unwrapathon, a hackathon hosted by Unwrap.ai, and our team won the prize for the addressing-misinformation track. We built a Chrome extension that fact-checks a health article by surfacing relevant research papers.

New Venture Fair 2023

For the second year in a row, my team was one of twenty selected to present at the New Venture Fair (NVC semi-finals) at UC Santa Barbara. Our product, SELie, helps children build emotion regulation skills. It was a great chance to pitch and gather feedback.

New Venture Fair 2022

I presented our idea at the New Venture Competition at UCSB. As a team of two, we built an app to help address under-resourced mental health support for university students using wearable and behavioral data. We were selected from twenty teams to move on to the next round.
